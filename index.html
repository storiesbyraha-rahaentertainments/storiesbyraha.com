I believe every wedding
is a story worth telling

Hi, I'm Raha — a wedding photographer with a passion for authentic moments. I don't direct, I observe. I don't pose, I wait. Because the best photographs happen when people forget the camera exists.

My style is warm, cinematic, and deeply human. Whether it's an intimate elopement or a grand celebration, I bring the same quiet dedication to every frame.

The Experience

What it's like to work with me

From the first message to the moment your gallery arrives, I want you to feel seen, heard, and genuinely excited. Here's how our journey together unfolds.

01

Inquiry & Connection Call

We start with a relaxed conversation — tell me about your day, your vision, your love story. No pressure, no forms.

02

Booking & Planning

Once we're a match, we lock in your date and I'll guide you through every detail — timeline, locations, lighting.

03

Your Wedding Day

I blend into the background, staying close to the moments that matter. You won't even know I'm there — until you see the photos.

04

Gallery Delivery

Within 6–8 weeks, your beautifully edited gallery arrives — ready to relive forever.
